What is Pokédex?

The Pokédex is an in-game encyclopedia or database in the Pokémon franchise, which records and provides information about the Pokémon species that a trainer encounters or captures. It serves as a guide to all known Pokémon, providing details such as their types, abilities, stats, evolutions, and more.

Here's a fun, emoji-based breakdown of what the Pokédex represents:

📖 Pokédex= Pokémon Encyclopedia

💥 Pokémon= The creatures you're trying to find and catch.

🔍 Search = Find specific Pokémon by name or type.

🗺️ Discover= Explore the world of Pokémon and encounter new species.

✨ Info= Learn detailed stats, abilities, evolutions, and much more!

❤️ Favorites= Add Pokémon to your personal collection of favorites.

🧑‍⚖️Trainer= The person using the Pokédex to catch and learn about Pokémon.

⚡Type= Each Pokémon has a type like Electric ⚡, Water 🌊, Fire 🔥, Grass 🌱, etc.

So, the Pokédex is essentially your **personal guide** to all things Pokémon as you embark on your journey to catch them all! 🐾
